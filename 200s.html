Starbucks nowadays.

Since the early 2000s, Starbucks has been growing more and more, with the creation of the frapuccinos, it kept calling even more costumers to all of the stores around the world. A important year for the brand was 2002, in which they improved their famous frapuccinos, to this day, the Caramel Frappuccino remains one of Starbucks' best-selling Frappuccino flavor. Once the Covid-19 pandamic hit the world, starbucks decided to close up to 400 stores in US and Canada, shifting their stores to a to go model, according to their CEO, starbucks was careful with the world's situation, but still wanted to provide a safe, familiar and convenient experience for their customers.

Today, starbucks coffee shop operates over 38,000 stores in 80 countries, it has transformed into a international coffee culture, but still keeps it unique identity in each store. "We are not in the coffee business serving people, We are people business serving coffee." said the CEO, Howard Schultz.
